Charity Begins At Home, So Does Your Fitness and Agility...

We provide daily workout routines and hand-hold you to fuel your motivation and make sure your form is right. You can build muscles and get fit at home without having to step out. You only need to dedicate a few minutes everyday, Monday to Friday. No expensive or specialised equipments needed (a pair of bottles of soft-drinks filled with water and may be a Skipping rope if it's available), all exercises can be performed with just your body weight.

Cardiovascular Training

cardiorespiratory endurance is the level at which your heart, your lungs, and your muscles work together. The more you keep them working the effectiveness of your fat loss develops. We will device the perfect blend to get you started and push you towards an atletic fitness.

Calisthenics

This is where we will use the weight we have gained around our waistline and else-where, to our advantage. We will push our own body-weight and become leaner and stronger as we progress gradually.

Mixed Martial Arts

You don't have to be a professional mixed martial arts (MMA) fighter to go a few rounds at home, but you might benefit from trying an MMA workout. This is a fantastic way of getting your heart-rate up while you get those muscles strong and shape-up.

Yoga

The benefits of Yoga are multifaceted. From increased strength to flexibility to heart health everything has its roots here. There is a reason it marks its presence in ancient scriptures, Yoga gets your blood flowing. More specifically, the relaxation exercises you learn in yoga can help your circulation, especially in your hands and feet. Yoga also gets more oxygen to your cells, which function better as a result. Join us to find out its magical healing powers...
